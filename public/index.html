TortoiseFit

What is TortoiseFit?

TortoiseFit is a fitness app tracker for people who are beginning their journey of a healthier lifestyle and is looking to start with low-impact exercises. Create a profile and track your weight and exercise at home with workout routine videos specifically catered to your needs. The best part of it all—it's completely free!

Who is TortoiseFit for?

This app was designed for people who are beginning their fitness journey and is looking for low-impact exercise routines, keep track of their weight, and find inspiration.

About Developers

The TortoiseFit app was developed as a group project for Georgia Institute of Technology's Full-Time Coding Boot Camp Fall 2017 Cohort.
